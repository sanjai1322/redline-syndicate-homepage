

# Redline Syndicate — Premium Performance Dealership Homepage

## Brand Identity
A dark, aggressive, motorsport-inspired website for a premium performance car dealership and tuning garage. Industrial aesthetic with cinematic photography, bold typography, and a serious automotive performance culture vibe.

## Color & Typography System
- **Backgrounds:** Matte deep black (#0B0B0C) and dark charcoal (#111111)
- **Accent:** Performance red (#C1121F) for CTAs and hover states
- **Text:** Soft white (#EAEAEA) with metallic silver accents
- **Headings:** Bold condensed uppercase (Bebas Neue style) — heavy motorsport feel
- **Body:** Clean sans-serif (Inter) — professional and readable

## Homepage Sections

### 1. Sticky Navigation Bar
- Left: "REDLINE SYNDICATE" logo in aggressive condensed typography with a minimal emblem
- Center links: Inventory · Builds · Performance · About · Contact
- Right: Red CTA button — "Explore Cars"
- Dark semi-transparent background that solidifies on scroll

### 2. Hero Section (Full-Screen Cinematic)
- Full-viewport hero with a dramatic dark garage background image (high-quality Porsche 911 GT3 RS on wet reflective concrete, moody overhead lighting)
- Dark gradient overlay for text readability
- Large uppercase heading: **ENGINEERED TO DOMINATE**
- Subtext: *Precision Performance. No Compromise.*
- Two buttons: Red "VIEW INVENTORY" and outlined "BOOK SERVICE"
- Subtle atmospheric haze effect

### 3. Featured Inventory (3-Card Grid)
- Dark background section with bold section heading
- Three premium car cards in a horizontal row
- Each card: high-res car image, model name, price, "View Details" button
- Matte black card backgrounds with thin red border on hover
- Subtle lift and scale animation on hover
- Generous luxury spacing

### 4. Signature Builds (Split Layout)
- Left side: Bold heading "SYNDICATE SIGNATURE BUILD", aggressive description about 700+ HP builds, precision tuning, custom exhaust systems, red CTA "Explore Builds"
- Right side: Large cinematic image of a modified Porsche in a performance garage
- Subtle carbon fiber texture overlay on the background

### 5. Performance Services (3 Service Blocks)
- Three minimal dark surface cards in a row
- Services: ECU Remapping · Turbo & Exhaust Upgrades · Full Performance Inspection
- Each block: minimal line icon, short description, red hover accent
- Clean, aggressive, no clutter

### 6. Visit the Garage (CTA Section)
- Large atmospheric section with dark garage interior background and warm lighting
- Location address, phone number, working hours
- Red CTA button: "SCHEDULE AN APPOINTMENT"

### 7. Footer
- Dark textured footer with four columns: Inventory · Performance · Company · Contact
- Minimal white social icons
- Clean, strong layout with brand mark

## Design Principles
- Ultra-clean layouts with strong spacing and visual hierarchy
- High-resolution realistic stock photography with cinematic lighting
- Smooth hover animations (scale, border color, opacity transitions)
- No glassmorphism, neon, or excessive gradients — pure automotive performance branding
- Fully responsive (desktop-first, mobile-friendly)
